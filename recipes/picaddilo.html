Classic Cuban-Style Picadillo Recipe

Description

Picadillo, is a traditional Latin America dish, and this Cuban version stands out for its unique sweet-and-savory flavor profile.

It's typically served with white rice, fried plantains, or sometimes used as a filling for empanadas or stuffed peppers.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion
  • ½ green bell pepper
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 6 large pitted green olives, quartered
  • ½ cup raisins
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon white suger
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat
  2. Cook and stir the chopped onion, bell pepper and garlic.
  3. Add ground beef into the skillet, cook and stir until crumbly and completely browned.
  4. Add the tomato sauce, olives, cumin, sazon seasoning, and salt into the ground beef mixture.
  5. Cover the skillet, cook under low heat until the mixture is through.
  6. Your dish is now ready, you can now serve it.
